Install AnglerOS

Flash AnglerOS onto a LILYGO T-Dongle-S3 straight from the browser. This build uses its TF card in 4-bit SDMMC mode.

LILYGO T-Dongle-S3 · 16MB flash

Requires Chrome or Edge. Plug the board into your PC, click Connect, then Install. If flashing doesn't start, hold the BOOT button while connecting to enter the bootloader.

After installing: connect to Wi-Fi
  1. A serial reconnect message after flashing can be ignored. AnglerOS uses the dongle's USB controller as the printer host after boot.
  2. Unplug the dongle from the PC and power it for normal use.
  3. Join the AnglerOS-Setup Wi-Fi network from a phone or laptop.
  4. Open http://192.168.4.1, choose your Wi-Fi network, and enter its password. The dongle reboots and joins it.
  5. The assigned IP address appears on the dongle's screen.

See the setup docs for details.
